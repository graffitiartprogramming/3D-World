"3D World" is a project created during Graffiti Art Programming Inc.â€™s st.ART Program in the summer of 2024 with participants from Ralph Brown Community Centre. 3D World turns Inner City kids into inspirers who reimagine their own community spaces through a blend of augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR). By merging the creative visions of youth, their drawings and designs become valuable assets, fueling the AR/VR experiences created together as they turn dreams into dreamscapes - imaginary landscapes that encapsulate the ambitious visions of neighborhood transformation. "3D World" is projected to be displayed under the VA railway underpass at The Forks in Winnipeg MB, estimated September 2024.
